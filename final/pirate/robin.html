She is the archaeologist for the Straw Hat Pirates. She is the seventh member of the crew and joined at the end of the Alabasta arc. She temporarily left the crew during the Water 7 arc, but rejoined during the Enies Lobby arc. Her dream is to find out the true history of the lost century by researching the poneglyphs. She is the sole survivor of the Ohara Clan, succeeding all of her predecessors as an archaeologist. She also consumed the Hana Hana no Mi a devil fruit that allows the user to replicate and sprout pieces of their body from the surface of any object or living thing.
