Also known as The Pirate Hunter, Roronoa Zoro was the first member to join the crew. He excels in sword art using the santoryu (three-swords) technique for which he uses both hands and mouth. His main goal is to become the best swordsman in the world. He's from Shimotsuki, located in the East Blue.
