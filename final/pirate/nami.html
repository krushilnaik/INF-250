Nami is the navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates. She is the third member of the crew and the second to join, officially doing so during the Arlong Park Arc. As navigator, Nami is responsible for evaluating the direction the ship is taking; she also often acts as the treasurer of the crew, managing the crew's money. She's from Cocoyasi Village, located in the East Blue.
