Monkey D. Luffy is the founder and captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. He's from the Foosha Village located in the East Blue. Reckless and hopeless in most topics, he relies on his crew members to help him fulfill his dream of becoming the Pirate King. To do so, he set sail to find the legendary treasure "One Piece", which belonged to the previous Pirate King, Gold Roger, before being executed. The treasure is stashed away in the most dangerous part of the world, the Grand Line.

Luffy ate the Gum-Gum Devil Fruit which made him basically Elastigirl.
