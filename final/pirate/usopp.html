Usopp is the sniper of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is the fourth member of the crew and the third to officially join. Despite leaving the crew in the Water 7 arc, he rejoined after the events at Ennies Lobby. His dream is to become a brave warrior of the seas like his father. He is from Syrup Village in the East Blue.
