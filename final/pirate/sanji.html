Sanji is the cook for the Straw Hat Pirates. His dream is to find the legendary sea known as All Blue, which, according to legend, is the only marine habitat in the world where all the species of fish that exist coincide. The main source of Sanji's strength and power comes from his legs since he insist in using his hands for cooking only. He's was born in the North Blue, but was raised in a sea restaurant in the East Blue.
